Hello, and welcome to The Maze!

My name is Eyas Ayesh, but most of my friends call me Iso (pronounced Ee-So). This is the personal website that I am building. As you can see it is far from complete, and, depending on when you are reading this, there may be nothing for you to see. Yet.

Like many of my favorite personal websites, The Maze is to be a metaphor. For me, it is about the way I experience ideas and interact with the world around me; navigating many twists and turns, runnning into deadends, and realizing I am not as smart as I may have thought.
